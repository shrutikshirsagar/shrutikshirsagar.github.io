Shruti Kshirsagar

I am a PhD student at MUSAE Lab, INRS-EMT. I am working on speech emotion recognition, domain adaptation for cross language emotion recognition, acoustic event detetction-sound detection projects under the supervision of Prof. Falk. My interest lies in signal processing, speech processing, speech prosody, deep learning and pattern recognition. I completed my Masters thesis from Digital Audio Processing Lab, IIT Bombay under the supervision of Prof. Preeti Rao, and I interned this Summer at Tamasek Laboratories in Nanyang Technological University, Singapore with Prof. Chng Eng Siong. I am currently working as a research intern (machine learning for audio analytic position) at Explainable Artificial Intelligence team , Bosch, California.
